Century Adventurers Arts & Sports Club presents MULLASSERY BLOCK PREMIER LEAGUE(MPL) 2021. MPL Auction was on 27-2-2021.MPL starts on 13th March 2021 and 3 days different matches of 12 teams takes place.The dates are 13,14 & 21 of March 2021.

Twelve franchises buy 168 players at MPL 2021 Player Auction

The CENTURY'S MPL 2021 Player Auction concluded in Mini Community hall Peruvallur with 168 players filling up the available slots.

The Twelve franchises went for some of the finest talent available, giving them a chance to unleash their potential and make an impact in the upcoming edition of the tournament.

The all-rounders were in demand. Nazeem was sold to Youngsters Venkidangu for a whopping points of 350 & became the most expensive player bought in the MPL Auction 2021.

Maneesh to Chithi Mullassery,Sreejith to Elamura Peruvallur,Saliq to Assured Warriors,Bineesh to Classic X1,Shameer to Youngsters Venkidangu, Vishnu to Team Paadi,Bestin to Mad Warriors,Sabu to Team Police Road,Sadiq to Blaze Padoor,Akhil to Battalion Mullassery,Sangeeth to Best X1 Karuvanthala,Jabbir Hussain to PCC Padoor were the key playerswho were retained by each teams.
